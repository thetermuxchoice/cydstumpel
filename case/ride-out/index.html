Ride Out —
The ultimate bike store

Client

Ride Out

Design by

Tim Borst

Awards

  • Honorable Mention — Awwwards
  • Mobile Excellence — Awwwards

An inspirational and experimental platform for any kind of cyclist. Ride Out is determined to be the coolest experience center in Europe.

Ride Out sets out to be more than a collection of sparkling bicycle brands. It creates a community through rides, talks, tryouts and other events.

The Ride Out shop is showcasing brands like Cervélo, Gazelle, Santa Cruz, Kalkhoff, Union, Focus and more. Lots of individual brands, all with their own original identity. Through neutral but experimental design, we were able to keep rideout.amsterdam clear and concise and at the same time daring and uncompromising.
